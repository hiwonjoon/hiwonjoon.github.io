About Me

Hello! I am first year Ph.D. Student at University of Texas at Austin. Fortunately, I got a chance to work with Prof. Scott Niekum. My reseach goal is finding a general solution that can handle real life problems; for instance, driving a car, brewing a coffee, organizing stuffs, or etc. During my journey to the goal, I would like to focus on deep learning to deal with ambiguity, which is the essence of those tasks. Hopefully, it could be directly applied to robots so that they let us focus on what we really need.
